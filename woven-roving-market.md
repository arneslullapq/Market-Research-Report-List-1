<p><p>The woven roving market comprises three primary types: single-end roving, multi-end roving, and chopped strands. Single-end roving features a single strand of fiberglass, providing high flexibility and strength for applications like roofing and boat manufacturing. Multi-end roving combines multiple strands, enhancing strength and durability, suitable for large-scale composite products. Chopped strands are short lengths of fiberglass, ideal for reinforcing plastics and other materials, offering versatility in products such as automotive parts and construction materials.</p></p>
