<p><ul><li>Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) Modules</li><li>Dual-mode Bluetooth Modules</li><li>Classic Bluetooth Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The computer Bluetooth modules market comprises three main types: Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) modules, Dual-mode Bluetooth modules, and Classic Bluetooth modules. BLE modules focus on energy efficiency, making them ideal for IoT devices that require long battery life. Dual-mode modules support both BLE and Classic Bluetooth, enabling compatibility with a wider range of devices. Classic Bluetooth modules offer robust data transfer capabilities, mainly for streaming audio and other high-bandwidth applications. Each type caters to specific use cases, enhancing connectivity options.</p></p>
