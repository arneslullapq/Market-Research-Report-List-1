<p><p>The Recovered Sulphur market consists of two main types: the Claus Process and Tail Gas Treatment. The Claus Process is primarily focused on converting hydrogen sulfide into elemental sulfur, providing a key source of recovered sulfur from natural gas and refining operations. Tail Gas Treatment enhances the Claus Process by treating the tail gases to further minimize emissions and maximize sulfur recovery. Together, they represent a vital segment in managing sulfur byproducts and promoting environmental sustainability in industrial processes.</p></p>
