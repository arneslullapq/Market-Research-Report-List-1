<p><strong>The Radiant Heating Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Radiation</li><li>Infrared Radiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiant heating panels market is primarily divided into two types: electric radiation and infrared radiation. Electric radiation panels utilize electrical resistance to generate heat, distributing warmth evenly across a space, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, infrared radiation panels emit heat in the form of infrared rays, directly warming objects and individuals in the room without heating the surrounding air, resulting in energy efficiency and comfort. Both types are gaining popularity for residential and commercial heating solutions.</p></p>
