<p><p>The vascular patches market is primarily categorized into two types: biologic vascular patches and synthetic vascular patches. Biologic vascular patches are derived from natural tissues and offer enhanced biocompatibility and lower rejection rates, making them suitable for patients with specific needs. In contrast, synthetic vascular patches are made from artificial materials, providing durability and consistency in performance, often used in broader surgical applications. Both types address vascular reconstruction, trauma repair, and other surgical demands in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
