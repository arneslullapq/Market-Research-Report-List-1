<p><p>The Azimuth and Tunnel Thrusters market includes two main types of propulsion systems used in maritime applications. Azimuth thrusters are directional propellers mounted on a vertical axle, providing enhanced maneuverability and thrust control for vessels. They are commonly used in dynamic positioning and offshore operations. Tunnel thrusters, on the other hand, are retractable or fixed thrusters installed within tunnels on the hull, offering lateral thrust to assist with docking and positioning. Both types enhance vessel agility and operational efficiency.</p></p>
