<p><p>The volumetric doser market includes devices that accurately measure and dispense liquids based on volume. The primary types are volumetric dosers and micro volumetric dosers. Volumetric dosers are designed for larger quantities, often used in industrial or commercial applications, ensuring consistent dosing for production processes. In contrast, micro volumetric dosers are specialized for precise measurements of smaller volumes, ideal for laboratory settings or applications requiring high accuracy in minimal quantities. Both types enhance efficiency and precision in fluid handling.</p></p>
