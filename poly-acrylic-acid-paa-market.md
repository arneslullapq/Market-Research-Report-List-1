<p><p>Poly Acrylic Acid (PAA) is a versatile polymer widely used in various industrial applications. The market for PAA is categorized into two main types: Solution PAA and Solid PAA. Solution PAA is typically used in applications requiring high viscosity and stability in liquid form, such as in adhesives, coatings, and personal care products. Each type serves distinct functions based on its physical properties.</p></p>
