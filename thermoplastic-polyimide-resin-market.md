<p><p>The thermoplastic polyimide resin market comprises two primary types: extrusion grade and molding grade. Extrusion grade is designed for continuous processing and is ideal for producing complex shapes like films, tubes, and profiles with high thermal and mechanical stability. In contrast, molding grade is tailored for specific applications such as injection or compression molding, offering enhanced flow properties and superior finish for intricate components. Both grades provide excellent high-temperature resistance, making them suitable for demanding industries like aerospace and electronics.</p></p>
