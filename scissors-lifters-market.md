<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Mechanical</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Scissor lifters are categorized into hydraulic, mechanical, and pneumatic types based on their power source and operational mechanism. Hydraulic scissor lifters utilize fluid pressure to lift heavy loads, offering high lifting capacities and smooth operation. Mechanical scissor lifters rely on mechanical systems like screws or gears for movement, providing more stability but limited lifting height. Pneumatic scissor lifters use compressed air, enabling quicker lifting and lowering actions, ideal for lighter loads in environments where speed is crucial.</p></p>
