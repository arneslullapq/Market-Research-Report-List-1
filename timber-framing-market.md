<p><p>The timber framing market comprises three main types: softwood, hardwood, and massive timber. Softwood, derived from coniferous trees, is lightweight, cost-effective, and commonly used for structural framing. Hardwood, sourced from deciduous trees, offers durability and aesthetic appeal, making it ideal for high-end applications and finishes. Massive timber, characterized by large timber sections, provides strength and sustainability benefits, often utilized in modern construction for its energy-efficient properties. Each type caters to specific architectural needs and design preferences.</p></p>
