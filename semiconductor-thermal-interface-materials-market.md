<p><p>The semiconductor thermal interface materials market includes various types designed to enhance thermal conductivity between components. Phase change materials adapt their phase to minimize thermal resistance, while thermal gap filler pads conform to surfaces to fill voids. Thermal putty pads offer flexibility and easy application, while thermal insulators provide effective barrier solutions. Thermal grease ensures optimal heat conduction through a viscous medium. Other materials may include specialized coatings or composites tailored for specific applications, enhancing overall thermal management in electronic devices.</p></p>
