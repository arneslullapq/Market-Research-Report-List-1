<p><p>The Food & Beverage Nitrogen Generators market primarily features two types: PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) and Membrane Nitrogen Generators. PSA generators use a process that separates nitrogen from other gases under high pressure, ensuring high purity nitrogen production, ideal for packaging and preserving food. In contrast, Membrane Nitrogen Generators utilize selective permeation through membranes to produce nitrogen, offering a simpler and more compact solution. Both technologies enhance food quality and extend shelf life, catering to the industry's specific needs.</p></p>
