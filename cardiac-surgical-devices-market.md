<p><p>The cardiac surgical devices market encompasses various technologies that support heart surgeries. Beating heart surgery systems allow operations while the heart continues to beat, reducing complications. Cardiopulmonary bypass equipment temporarily takes over the heart and lung functions, maintaining blood circulation and oxygenation during surgery. Cardiac ablation devices are used to treat arrhythmias by destroying problematic heart tissue. Perfusion disposables, including tubing and filters, facilitate the safe transport of blood and fluids during surgical procedures, ensuring effective patient management.</p></p>
