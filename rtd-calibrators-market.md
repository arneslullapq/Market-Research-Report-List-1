<p><strong>The RTD Calibrators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Input Mode</li><li>Output Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RTD calibrators market is categorized into two main types: input mode and output mode. Input mode calibrators measure resistance temperature detectors (RTDs) by simulating temperature inputs, allowing users to verify and calibrate sensing devices accurately. In contrast, output mode calibrators generate a specific temperature reading, which is then compared against the RTD's actual output. Both modes are essential for ensuring the precision and reliability of temperature measurements in various industrial and laboratory applications, supporting quality control and compliance.</p></p>
