<p><p>The Cristobalite market comprises two main types: sand form and milled flour form. Sand form Cristobalite is a naturally occurring crystalline silica used primarily in construction, ceramics, and filtration applications due to its durability and high-temperature resistance. Milled flour form Cristobalite, on the other hand, is finely ground and utilized in specialized industries such as coatings, plastics, and rubber, enhancing product strength and thermal properties. Both forms serve distinct purposes, catering to various industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
