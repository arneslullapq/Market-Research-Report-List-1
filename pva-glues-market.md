<p><p>PVA (Polyvinyl Acetate) glues are categorized into two main market types: Type I and Type II. Type I PVA glues are water-resistant and suitable for exterior applications, making them ideal for outdoor projects. Type II PVA glues offer better water resistance and are often used in high-moisture environments, such as kitchens and bathrooms. Both types are popular in woodworking, crafts, and educational applications, catering to different needs based on the desired durability and environmental conditions.</p></p>
