<p><strong>The GPS Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SAW Filters</li><li>BAW Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS filters market primarily consists of two types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters. SAW filters utilize surface wave technology to achieve high frequency selectivity, making them ideal for consumer electronics and mobile devices. In contrast, BAW filters use volume waves, offering superior performance in terms of size and temperature stability, making them suitable for advanced applications like satellite communications. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing GPS signal accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
