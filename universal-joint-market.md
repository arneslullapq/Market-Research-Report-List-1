<p><strong>The Universal Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Velocity Joint</li><li>Cross-axis Universal Joint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Universal Joint (U-Joint) market is primarily divided into two types: Constant Velocity Joints (CVJs) and Cross-axis Universal Joints. CVJs offer smooth rotational motion with minimal vibration and are commonly used in applications where consistent angles are crucial, such as in drive shafts for vehicles. On the other hand, Cross-axis Universal Joints are designed to handle larger angles and are typically utilized in heavy machinery and industrial applications where robustness and flexibility are essential for efficient power transmission.</p></p>
