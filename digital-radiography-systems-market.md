<p><p>Digital Radiography Systems encompass two primary market types: Computed Radiography (CR) and Digital Radiography (DR). CR technology uses imaging plates to capture X-ray data, which is then scanned and digitized, providing a bridge from traditional film to digital formats. Conversely, DR technology directly converts X-ray images into digital signals, offering faster processing times and superior image quality. Both systems enhance diagnostic capabilities and workflow efficiency in medical imaging, catering to diverse clinical needs and facility budgets.</p></p>
