<p><ul><li>Step-up Transformer</li><li>Step-down Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric transformer market is divided into step-up and step-down transformers. Step-up transformers increase voltage levels, adapting low voltage inputs for applications like electric vehicles and portable devices, enhancing efficiency in power delivery. Conversely, step-down transformers reduce voltage, making them suitable for consumer electronics and portable devices that require lower voltages. Both types leverage piezoelectric materials to achieve high efficiency, compact designs, and lightweight characteristics, driving their demand across various industries, including automotive, telecommunications, and medical devices.</p></p>
