<p><p>Eye tracking AR glasses are categorized into monocular and binocular types based on their design and functionality. Monocular eye tracking glasses feature a single lens and primarily track the user's gaze from one eye, offering a compact form factor and lightweight design. In contrast, binocular eye tracking glasses utilize dual lenses, capturing gaze data from both eyes, which enhances depth perception and accuracy. This distinction influences applications and user experiences, catering to diverse needs in augmented reality environments.</p></p>
