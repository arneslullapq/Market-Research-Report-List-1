<p><strong>The Duct Smoke Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Duct smoke detectors are essential for HVAC systems, detecting smoke in air ducts to prevent fire hazards. The market primarily features two types: photoelectric and ionization detectors. Photoelectric detectors use a light source and a photosensitive sensor to identify smoke particles, making them effective for smoldering fires. In contrast, ionization detectors employ radioactive material to sense ion changes in the air, which works well for flaming fires. Both types are crucial for enhancing safety in commercial and residential environments.</p></p>
