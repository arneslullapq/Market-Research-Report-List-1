<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Digital Radiography Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Direct Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>Computed radiography (CR) Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital radiography detectors can be classified into three main types: </p><p>1. **Indirect Flat Panel Detectors** use a scintillator to convert X-rays into visible light, which is then detected by photodiodes, offering high image quality but slower processing times.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Direct Flat Panel Detectors** directly convert X-rays into electrical signals, providing faster imaging and reduced exposure times.</p><p>3. **Computed Radiography (CR) Detectors** employ photostimulable phosphor plates to capture X-rays, which are later processed by a laser reader, making them a cost-effective solution with moderate image quality.</p></p>
