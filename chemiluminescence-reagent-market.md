<p><p>The chemiluminescence reagent market encompasses three main types: indirect chemiluminescence, direct chemiluminescence, and electrochemiluminescence. Indirect chemiluminescence involves the use of secondary agents to enhance luminescent signals, often for improved sensitivity in assays. Direct chemiluminescence involves a direct reaction that emits light without additional agents, making it simpler and faster for certain applications. Electrochemiluminescence combines electrochemical reactions with luminescent emissions, offering high sensitivity and specificity for detecting analytes in various fields, including clinical diagnostics and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
