<p><strong>The COVID-19 Diagnostic Tests Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 diagnostic tests market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT)** enable quick results, often within minutes, by using lateral flow assays for antigen or antibody detection, making them suitable for mass screening. </p><p>2. **Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA)** are laboratory-based tests that provide more detailed serological data by measuring antibodies or antigens in samples, suitable for confirming infections or immunological responses.</p><p>3. **Neutralization Assays** assess the effectiveness of antibodies in inhibiting virus activity, crucial for understanding immunity and vaccine efficacy.</p></p>
