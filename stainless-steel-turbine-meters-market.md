<p><ul><li>Threaded Connection</li><li>Flanged Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stainless steel turbine meters are used for measuring fluid flow and are available in two main connection types: threaded and flanged. Threaded connection meters utilize male or female threads, providing easy installation in pipe systems with limited space; they are typically suitable for smaller pipe sizes. Flanged connection meters, on the other hand, use flanges for mechanical connection, offering greater stability and supporting larger pipe sizes and higher pressure applications, making them ideal for industrial environments.</p></p>
