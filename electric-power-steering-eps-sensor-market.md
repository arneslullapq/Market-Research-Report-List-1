<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering EPS Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Torque Only Sensor(TOS)</li><li>Torque Angle Sensor(TAS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) sensor market includes key components such as Torque Only Sensors (TOS) and Torque Angle Sensors (TAS). TOS measures the steering torque directly, providing feedback for precise steering assistance, enhancing vehicle control. In contrast, TAS monitors the angle of the steering wheel, allowing for adjustments based on the driver's input and vehicle dynamics. Both sensor types are crucial for improving driving safety and comfort, contributing to the increasing demand for advanced steering technologies in modern vehicles.</p></p>
