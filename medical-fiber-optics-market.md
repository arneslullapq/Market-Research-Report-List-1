<p><ul><li>Silica Fiber/Glass Fiber Optics</li><li>Plastic Fiber Optics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical fiber optics market comprises two main types: silica fiber optics and plastic fiber optics. Silica fiber optics, made from glass, are known for their high performance, providing excellent light transmission and durability, making them ideal for applications like minimally invasive surgeries and endoscopy. In contrast, plastic fiber optics are lighter and more flexible, offering ease of handling and lower manufacturing costs, which can be advantageous in certain applications, such as dental and veterinary procedures. Both types play crucial roles in advancing medical technologies.</p></p>
